Tools for thermodynamic and electrochemical phase stability analysis.

| [pourbaix-diagram](pourbaix-diagram/SKILL.md) | Pourbaix (potential-pH) diagrams for electrochemical stability. Identifies corrosion, passivation, and immunity regions using pymatgen PourbaixDiagram. |
